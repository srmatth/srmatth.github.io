    Reading

    There is not much I enjoy more than tearing through a really good book. I’m a fan of good writing, whether it is fiction or nonfiction, entertainment or informational. Please email me any recommendations, I love reading books recommended to me. On that note, my most recent high recommendations are: The Comfort Crisis by Michael Easter, Antifragile by Nassim Nicholas Taleb, American Prometheus by Kai Bird and Martin Sherwin, and The Three Body Problem by Cixin Liu. And if you want something entirely entertaining, the Murderbot series by Martha Wells is fantastic.

    Currently this is just a list of everything I’ve read since April of 2020, without any thoughts or commentary. While I hope to change that in the future, reach out if you want to know my thoughts on a book, I love to discuss them!
